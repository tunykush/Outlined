The rules of IEEE, encoded.

Numbered references in brackets, initials-first author format, and a placeholder [1] users renumber by first appearance in their final document.

01

Bracketed numbers

Each in-text citation is a bracketed number: [1], [2], [3]… Renumber by first appearance when finalising your reference list.

As shown in [1].
02

Initials-first authors

IEEE writes the initials before the surname: J. A. Smith. Use "and" before the last author; up to 6 authors then et al.

J. A. Smith, T. Nguyen, and R. K. Patel
03

Quoted article titles

Article titles are placed in straight double quotes; journal/conference names italicised.

"On methods of citation," Journal of Library Studies
04

DOI form

IEEE accepts doi: 10.… or the full https form. We default to the latter.

doi: 10.1234/jls.2024.0045
05

Renumbering note

Outlined emits [1] as a placeholder. The metascan adds a note reminding you to renumber by final order of appearance.

Note: renumber references by first appearance.
