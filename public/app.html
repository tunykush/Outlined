    With or without DOI, volume, issue, page range or article number.

    📰

    News / magazine

    Online and print. Author, date, section handled automatically.

    📝

    📚

    Watch it work

    Auto-fetch fills the fields for you.

    Drop in a DOI, journal URL, or news article. The server fetches the page, reads JSON-LD, Open Graph, and Dublin Core metadata, and pre-populates every field. You review, confirm the source type, and copy.
