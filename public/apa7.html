The rules of APA 7th, encoded.

Author shape, title sentence case, italics, DOI prefix, and the article–number rule for online journals — every line below is implemented in src/shared/citation/styles/apa7.ts.

01

Author format

Family, F. M. — comma-then-initials. Use ampersand before the final author. List up to 20 authors; for 21+, list 19, ellipsis, last.

02

Title case

Reference list titles use sentence case — capitalize first word, first word after colon, and proper nouns only. Italicise the journal/site name, not the article title.

03

DOI prefix

DOIs are normalised to https://doi.org/… and never end in a full stop. doi:… form is rewritten by APA metascan.

04

Article number rule

Online journals without page ranges may use an article number. APA emits "Article 12" in place of pages.
