How it works

A small pipeline with citation rules at the end.

Outlined keeps the interface calm, but the workflow is strict: fetch safely, parse metadata, infer source type, and render against style-specific rules.

01

Read the page

Public URLs are fetched with SSRF protection, timeout limits, and a capped response size.

02

Parse metadata

JSON-LD, citation tags, Open Graph, Dublin Core, and common page fields are scanned in order.

03

Infer the source

The app proposes a source type, then lets you refine the fields before rendering the reference.

04

Apply the style

APA 7th, RMIT Harvard, and IEEE output is generated from separate rule modules.

Supported styles

Designed around citation details that are easy to miss.

Author counts, DOI normalisation, article numbers, publisher omission, and in-text variants are encoded as style behavior instead of loose templates.
